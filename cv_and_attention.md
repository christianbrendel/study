- 2018-06-15 [Image Transformer](https://arxiv.org/abs/1802.05751): Applying the transformer architecture on images: Conditional image generation and image super resolution task.

- 2019-05-16 [Squeeze-and-Excitation Networks](https://arxiv.org/pdf/1709.01507): Introduction of SE-layers that recalibrates filter responses in two steps: First each filter is squeezed by global avaging pooling (thereby increasing the receptive field). After that a two-layer FFN uses these scores to calculate scores the filter respones are weighted with.
